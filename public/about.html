About LifeLink

Why we exist and how it all works.

🩸 Our Mission

Every year, millions of lives depend on timely access to blood. Yet blood banks frequently run out of critical blood types, and patients in need struggle to find donors quickly.

LifeLink was built to close that gap — connecting willing donors, hospitals, and patients in one simple platform. No complicated process. No waiting. Just faster, more human connections when they matter most.

👥 Who We Serve

Blood Donors — Individuals aged 18–65 who are healthy and willing to donate. LifeLink helps them register, tracks their eligibility window (90 days between donations), and connects them with people in need near them.

Hospitals & Blood Banks — Medical institutions can register their facility, list their blood stock levels, and post urgent requirements. Patients and donors can see real-time availability.

Patients & Families — Anyone facing a medical emergency can post a blood request. The request appears publicly so eligible donors can step forward.

⏱ The 90-Day Eligibility Rule

Whole blood donors must wait at least 90 days (3 months) between donations. This is a medical requirement to allow the body to fully replenish its blood supply.

When a donor registers on LifeLink, they enter their last donation date. The system automatically calculates whether they are eligible to donate again. If not, it tells them exactly when they will be eligible.

After a recorded donation, the 90-day clock starts fresh. This ensures donor health is always protected.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

▶ Is my personal data safe on LifeLink?
Yes. Donor emails are never shown publicly. Only the name, blood type, and city are visible to others searching for donors.
▶ Can I donate more frequently than 90 days?
No. Whole blood donation requires a 90-day gap for safety. For platelet or plasma donations, the rules differ and are handled separately by hospitals.
▶ How do hospitals update their blood stock?
Hospitals registered on LifeLink can update their stock levels via the API endpoint PUT /api/hospitals/:id/stock. We are working on a hospital dashboard for easier updates.
▶ What happens after I donate?
A hospital or admin can record your donation using the donor ID. Your last donation date updates, and the 90-day window starts again. You will appear as "not yet eligible" to other users until the window passes.
▶ Is LifeLink free to use?
Yes. LifeLink is completely free for donors, patients, and hospitals.
