Who Can Donate?

  • Age 18 to 65 years
  • Weight above 50 kg
  • Haemoglobin ≥ 12.5 g/dL
  • No fever or cold in past 2 weeks
  • No tattoo/piercing in past 6 months
  • Not pregnant or nursing

⏱ 90-Day Rule

Whole blood donors must wait 90 days (3 months) between donations. LifeLink automatically checks your last donation date and tells you when you're eligible again.

Blood Types

  • O- — Universal donor
  • AB+ — Universal recipient
  • O+ — Most common
  • AB- — Rarest type
